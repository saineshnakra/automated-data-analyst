# Code of conduct

## Our standard

ADA welcomes contributors of every background and experience level. Be respectful, specific, and generous when discussing code or analytical choices. Critique the work, not the person. Assume good intent while still being clear about accuracy, privacy, and product quality.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discrimination, threats, sexualized attention, publishing another person's private information, or sustained disruption of project discussion.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately through the contact information at [sainesh.com](https://sainesh.com/). Reports will be reviewed promptly and kept confidential as far as practical. Maintainers may edit, remove, or reject contributions and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation when behavior threatens a safe, productive community.

## Scope

This policy applies in repository discussions, issues, pull requests, reviews, and public spaces where someone is representing ADA.

This policy is informed by the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).
